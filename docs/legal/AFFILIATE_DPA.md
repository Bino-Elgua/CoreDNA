# Data Processing Agreement — CoreDNA Affiliate Hub

**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-08
**Last Updated:** 2026-01-08

## 1. Definitions

- **"Partner"** = You, the individual or entity operating the affiliate referral page
- **"CoreDNA"** = CoreDNA, Inc. (the data processor)
- **"Visitor"** = End user who visits Partner's affiliate landing page
- **"Personal Data"** = Any information relating to a visitor
- **"Processing"** = Any operation performed on visitor data (collection, storage, analysis)

## 2. Roles & Responsibilities

### Partner's Role (Data Controller)
As the data controller, you are responsible for:
- ✓ Obtaining valid, informed consent from visitors
- ✓ Providing clear privacy notices before tracking
- ✓ Complying with all applicable data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- ✓ Responding to visitor rights requests (access, deletion, portability)
- ✓ Honoring opt-out requests within 48 hours
- ✓ Maintaining records of all consents
- ✓ Notifying authorities of any data breaches

### CoreDNA's Role (Data Processor)
CoreDNA agrees to:
- ✓ Process visitor data only per your written instructions
- ✓ Implement appropriate security measures (encryption, access controls)
- ✓ Not share data with third parties except as authorized
- ✓ Assist with visitor rights requests
- ✓ Delete data upon your instruction or visitor opt-out
- ✓ Maintain audit logs of all access and processing
- ✓ Comply with applicable data protection regulations

## 3. Scope of Processing

### Data Collected
- **Visitor IP address** – for company identification via Clearbit
- **Company name** – derived from IP via Clearbit
- **Consent preferences** – visitor's selection of company ID / marketing / sales
- **Consent timestamp** – exact moment consent was given
- **Referral status** – whether visitor converted to customer

### Data NOT Collected
- ❌ Personal email addresses (unless visitor consents to sales)
- ❌ Phone numbers
- ❌ Names or titles
- ❌ LinkedIn profiles or URLs
- ❌ Any biometric data
- ❌ Sensitive personal data

### Processing Purposes
- Identify companies visiting the affiliate page
- Send marketing updates (if consented)
- Enable personalized outreach (if consented)
- Track referral conversions for commission calculation
- Maintain audit trails for compliance

### Legal Basis
- **Consent** – for all visitor tracking (GDPR Article 6(1)(a))
- **Legitimate Interest** – for affiliate commission calculation
- **Legal Obligation** – for maintaining audit logs

## 4. Data Protection Measures

### Security Controls
CoreDNA implements:
- ✓ Encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.2+)
- ✓ Encryption of data at rest (AES-256)
- ✓ Role-based access control (RBAC)
- ✓ Regular security audits and penetration testing
- ✓ Incident response procedures
- ✓ Backup and disaster recovery

### Data Retention
- **Visitor logs** – retained for 12 months, then deleted
- **Consent records** – retained per Partner's legal obligations
- **Opt-out requests** – processed and deleted within 48 hours
- **Audit logs** – retained for 2 years for compliance

### Sub-Processors
CoreDNA uses the following sub-processors (with your consent):
- **Clearbit** – for company identification via IP
- **Supabase** – for data storage (encrypted)
- **AWS** – for infrastructure and backups

Partner acknowledges and approves these sub-processors.

## 5. Visitor Rights

### Right to Access (GDPR Article 15)
Visitors may request access to their data via `coredna.ai/opt-out`. CoreDNA will provide a summary within 30 days.

### Right to Deletion (GDPR Article 17)
Visitors may request deletion via the opt-out page. CoreDNA will anonymize/delete within 48 hours.

### Right to Portability (GDPR Article 20)
Visitors may request their data in machine-readable format via privacy@coredna.ai.

### Right to Object (GDPR Article 21)
Visitors may object to processing at any time via the opt-out page.

### Right to Rectification (GDPR Article 16)
Visitors may request corrections to their company name or other data.

## 6. Partner Obligations

By accepting this DPA, Partner agrees to:

1. **Lawful Basis** – Obtain valid consent before any tracking
2. **Transparency** – Provide clear privacy notices disclosing:
   - CoreDNA as processor
   - Clearbit for company identification
   - 20% commission arrangement
   - Opt-out mechanism
3. **Consent Management** – Maintain records of all consents
4. **Opt-Out Compliance** – Honor all opt-out requests within 48 hours
5. **No Dark Patterns** – Avoid deceptive practices (pre-checked boxes, confusing language)
6. **Data Minimization** – Don't collect more data than necessary
7. **Sub-Processor Notification** – Inform visitors about Clearbit usage
8. **Breach Notification** – Notify CoreDNA of any suspected breaches
9. **Self-Scouting Prevention** – Don't use the platform to track your own employees/domain
10. **No Sale of Data** – Don't sell or share visitor data with third parties

## 7. Prohibited Activities

Partner agrees NOT to:
- ❌ Collect personal data without consent
- ❌ Use the platform for self-scouting (tracking own domain)
- ❌ Sell or share visitor data
- ❌ Send unsolicited spam or harassment
- ❌ Scrape or bulk-export visitor data
- ❌ Use data for purposes other than referral marketing
- ❌ Misrepresent the affiliate relationship

Violations will result in immediate suspension of affiliate privileges.

## 8. Indemnification

### Partner Indemnifies CoreDNA
Partner agrees to indemnify CoreDNA from any claims arising from:
- Partner's violation of this DPA
- Partner's violation of applicable data protection laws
- Partner's collection of data without valid consent
- Partner's non-compliance with visitor rights requests

### CoreDNA Indemnifies Partner
CoreDNA agrees to indemnify Partner from claims arising from:
- CoreDNA's violation of this DPA
- CoreDNA's security breach
- CoreDNA's misuse of data beyond Partner's instructions

- CoreDNA will delete all visitor data within 30 days
- Audit logs retained for 12 months for compliance purposes
- Affiliate privileges revoked
